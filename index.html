On this website you can browse old TibiaNews.net articles, dating back over two decades ago.

Use the very primitive search function down below, or download the full archive (8 MB) and create your own search function.

All the source code for this website, including the archiving tool, can be found on GitHub.

Links posted in articles, including images, can not be guaranteed to work today. Simply use this archive to browse through the text of the articles.

All links to the articles in this archive can be found here (old TibiaNews) and here ("new" TibiaNews, after they redesigned the website).

TibiaNews.net was the largest Tibia news fansite in existence.
It was frequently visited by thousands of Tibians every month and
included a ton of articles, interviews and research papers about the game Tibia.
It shutdown many years ago and the content posted there has, until this day,
not been searchable in an easy way.
